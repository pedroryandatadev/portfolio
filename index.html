Pedro Ryan

Data Scientist and Analyst

I'm a data scientist and analyst with a degree in Systems Analysis and Development. I combine technical expertise in data with a software development mindset, which allows me to build complete solutions from raw data to final delivery.

In my day-to-day work, I use Python, Scikit-learn, and API integration to model problems, automate processes, and turn data into decisions. I pay special attention to the usability of my solutions, because it's not enough for something to work: it has to make sense to the people using it.

I also have a strong interest in academic research, believing that well-applied data has the power to generate knowledge with real impact.

Most of my projects are free to reuse under their respective licenses, except for those developed as scientific proposals, which were created to contribute to academic knowledge rather than for general use.

My interests span data science, analysis, software architecture, and automation. Beyond the technical side, I care about how projects are presented — I believe a well-built solution deserves to be communicated with the same level of quality.
